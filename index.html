Nuclear Weapons

Nuclear weapons are incredibly powerful bombs that use reactions within atomic nuclei to create massive explosions. They cause huge destruction, release dangerous radiation, and have long-lasting effects on the environment. Think of them as the most powerful and dangerous bombs humans have ever created.

Issues

Causes of Nuclear weapons

Nuclear weapons are developed to prevent other countries from attacking by posing a threat of massive destruction. They also give countries more influence and power in global politics.

Issues

Solution of Nuclear weapons

To prevent nuclear weapons, countries must sign agreements to stop their spread, work together to eliminate existing ones, and promote peaceful conflict resolution.>
